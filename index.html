About Organization

Aarambha Foundation is a non-profit organization based in Kathmandu, Nepal, dedicated to improving education, health, and environmental conditions for underprivileged children. Established in 2015, it works to ensure that motivated children can access quality education despite financial or social barriers. The Foundation’s mission is to spread the light of education and build empowered, healthy, and sustainable communities across Nepal.

"Mission & Vision"
